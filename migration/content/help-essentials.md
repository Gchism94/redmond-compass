# Help & Essentials

Where to turn for food, housing, health, and crisis support across Redmond, Terrebonne, and Crooked River Ranch. Free and low-cost help from people in our community.

Not sure where to begin? Dial 211. Oregon's free help line. Tell them what you need (food, rent, utilities, childcare, healthcare) and they connect you to local resources. Call 211, text your ZIP code to 898211, or visit 211info.org. Help is available in many languages.

## Crisis & Safety

Available 24/7

### 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Free, confidential support any time, for anyone. Veterans press 1.

Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

### Deschutes County Crisis Line

Mental health crisis support for all ages, regardless of ability to pay.

### Stabilization Center (Walk-in)

Walk in any time to see a therapist. No appointment, no cost, all ages.

### Saving Grace Helpline

Safety, shelter, and advocacy for survivors of domestic violence, sexual assault, and stalking.

24-hour helpline, 365 days a year.

### Alcohol & Drug Helpline

Crisis support, treatment referrals, and substance-use information.

Text 'RecoveryNow' to 839863.

### Oregon YouthLine

Teen-to-teen support. Trained teens answer 4 to 10 pm; adults answer all other hours.

Text 'teen2teen' to 839863.

## Food

No one goes hungry

### NeighborImpact Food Program

The hub for food pantries across Redmond and Central Oregon. They will point you to the nearest pantry.

### St. Vincent de Paul, Redmond

Food pantry and emergency assistance for Redmond-area neighbors.

### Shepherd's House Redmond Center

Daily meals, open to anyone in need.

### Meals for Adults 60+

Meals on Wheels and community dining through the Redmond Senior Center and Council on Aging.

## Housing & Shelter

A safe place to stay

### Shepherd's House Redmond Center

24-hour shelter with meals, case management, showers, and laundry. The only Central Oregon shelter with walk-in family rooms.

### Bethlehem Inn, Redmond

Emergency shelter and basic needs. Walk-ins welcome.

### NeighborImpact Housing Help

Rent assistance, eviction prevention, and homeless services as funds allow.

### Central Oregon Shelter Listing

The current list of warming and emergency shelters across the region, kept up to date by the Homeless Leadership Coalition.

## Help Paying Bills

When money is tight

### Energy & Heating Assistance

NeighborImpact helps with heating and utility bills and can stop a shut-off. Call the energy hotline.

### Rent & Utility Help

NeighborImpact rental assistance, water and utility help, and referrals.

### St. Vincent de Paul, Redmond

Help with utilities and other one-time emergencies.

## Health & Medical

Care for everyone

### Mosaic Community Health, Redmond

Medical, dental, behavioral health, and pharmacy on a sliding fee scale. All are welcome, whatever your insurance status.

### Deschutes County Public Health

Immunizations, WIC, family planning, and screenings. Sliding fees available.

### Oregon Health Plan (OHP)

Free or low-cost health coverage. Mosaic's enrollment team can help you apply.

## Mental Health & Recovery

You are not alone

### Deschutes County Behavioral Health

Counseling and treatment for mental health and substance use, all ages.

### BestCare Treatment Services

Substance use treatment and outpatient counseling, Redmond office.

### St. Charles Behavioral Health

Behavioral health services connected to the regional hospital system.

In a crisis right now? You do not have to wait for an appointment. Reach a trained person any time.

## Older Adults

Adults 60 and over

### Council on Aging of Central Oregon

The regional hub for aging services across Central Oregon. Their Help Desk connects you to Meals on Wheels, free Medicare counseling, caregiver support, in-home help, and more.

Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM.

### Redmond Senior Center

The local hub for Redmond seniors. Delivers Meals on Wheels, runs community dining and social activities, and offers Passion for Pets, which provides pet food to homebound Meals on Wheels clients.

Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, closed weekends.

## Veterans

For those who served

### Deschutes County Veterans' Services

Free help with VA benefits and claims for veterans and their families. Redmond office by appointment on Thursdays.

### Veterans Crisis Line

Confidential support from people who understand military life.

Text 838255. Available 24/7.

## Families & Children

For parents and kids

### Family Access Network (FAN)

Advocates based in local schools connect families with food, clothing, housing, and other essentials.

Contact your child's school office.

### WIC Nutrition Program

Food, nutrition support, and resources for pregnant people, new parents, and children under 5.

### Oregon YouthLine

A safe place for young people to talk through anything, big or small.

Text 'teen2teen' to 839863.

We check this information regularly, but hours and services do change. Please call ahead before you visit. If a listing looks out of date, let us know and we will fix it.
