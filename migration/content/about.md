# The Redmond Compass

Created with one simple goal: to help people discover, connect with, and support the businesses, organizations, events, and people that make Redmond such a special place to live.

## Welcome to The Redmond Compass

As Redmond continues to grow, it can be difficult to keep up with everything happening around town. New businesses open, community events are announced, nonprofits need support, local organizations are making a difference, and residents are constantly searching for trusted local resources.

The Redmond Compass was built to bring all of those things together in one place.

Whether you're a lifelong resident, new to the area, visiting Central Oregon, or a local business owner — we want this platform to help you find what you're looking for and discover things you didn't even know existed.

## Why We Created It

The Redmond Compass was created because our community deserves a dedicated resource focused entirely on Redmond and the surrounding area. Our goal is to:

- Support local businesses by helping residents discover and connect with them.

- Promote community events, fundraisers, festivals, and local activities.

- Highlight nonprofits, volunteer opportunities, and organizations making a positive impact.

- Share local resources that help residents navigate daily life.

- Celebrate the people, places, and stories that make Redmond unique.

- Strengthen community connections by creating a central hub for information and engagement.

We believe strong communities are built when neighbors support neighbors and local businesses thrive.

### Our Mission

To connect the people of Redmond with the businesses, organizations, events, and resources that strengthen our community — while encouraging local discovery, engagement, and support.

### Our Purpose

The Redmond Compass exists to make it easier for residents and visitors to find trusted local information, support local businesses, participate in community events, and stay connected to everything happening in and around Redmond.

We believe local communities are strongest when information is accessible, businesses are supported, and people feel connected to where they live.
