### Cascade Custom Creations

Cascade Custom Creations is a locally owned Redmond business specializing in high-quality custom laser engraving and personalized products. From minimalist RFID-blocking wallets and metal business cards to engraved tumblers, mugs, and custom gifts, every piece is crafted with precision and attention to detail. Whether you’re looking for a unique personal item, a meaningful gift, or branded products for your business, Cascade Custom Creations brings your ideas to life. They also offer engraving on customer-provided items, making it easy to create something truly one-of-a-kind. Known for quality craftsmanship and a personal touch, Cascade Custom Creations is a go-to for custom work that stands out.

![Herringbone Bookstore](https://media.base44.com/images/public/6a05e41957c8ee753cb7380c/87537c234_HerringboneBooks.jpg)

### Herringbone Bookstore

Located in the heart of downtown Redmond, Herringbone Books is an independent, family-friendly bookstore offering a thoughtfully curated selection of bestselling books, local and regional authors, children's books, gifts, games, and unique treasures for readers of all ages. More than just a bookstore, Herringbone Books serves as a community gathering place, hosting author events, book clubs, and special literary experiences that bring readers together. Whether you're searching for your next favorite novel, a gift for a fellow book lover, or a welcoming place to explore, Herringbone Books is a local gem worth discovering.

### Wilson's of Redmond

Central Oregon's largest furniture and mattress destination, proudly serving the region since 1962. A 24,000 sqft multi-building showroom with 2,500 items in stock, including living room, bedroom, dining, and office furniture and mattresses from brands like Accord Comfort and Copperrest.
